Globounty Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 21, 2026

1. Who We Are

Globounty (“we,” “us”) is a peer-to-peer task board that helps users post tasks and connect with others. We do not process payments, provide escrow, verify work, or participate in financial transactions between users. All payments are arranged off-app at users’ own risk.

Payment labels shown in the App (such as “Escrow” or “To Be Negotiated”) describe user preferences only—not services we provide.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account: email, authentication identifiers (Supabase Auth), optional Google/Apple sign-in data
  • Profile: nickname (one-time, locked after confirmation), disclaimer acceptance, suspension status
  • Content: task title, description, reward text (display only), category, payment preference label, claim status
  • Chat: text messages only while a task is actively matched
  • Reports: report category and timestamp
  • Device/ads (mobile): advertising ID via Google AdMob; device identifier used only for abuse prevention when you delete your account
  • Security logs: IP address on certain events (e.g., account deletion), retained up to 6 months

We do not collect profile photos. We do not sell personal information.

3. How We Use Information

  • Operate posting, claiming, chat, and reporting features
  • Enforce limits and prevent abuse (including account suspension)
  • Show ads via Google AdMob on supported mobile devices
  • Comply with law and defend against legal claims

We do not use the App to collect or process payment card numbers, bank credentials, or transaction amounts.

4. Chat & Platform Responsibility

We do not routinely monitor private messages, do not guarantee user identity or reliability, and do not mediate disputes. Chat for a task is deleted when the match ends (release, withdraw, or completion). Use the Report button if you suspect abuse.

5. Third-Party Processors

  • Supabase (auth, database, hosting — US and other regions)
  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign In (optional login)

Data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS. We do not provide end-to-end encrypted messaging.

6. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You may request access, correction, deletion, or export by emailing houbaibai688@gmail.com (subject: “Privacy Request”). California residents: we do not sell personal information; you may limit ad tracking via device settings.

7. Data Deletion

Upon your request, we permanently delete your account, profile, posted tasks, and chat messages immediately from our active systems.

To comply with legal obligations under GDPR Article 17(3)(e) and the EU Digital Services Act (DSA), we retain anonymized records that cannot be traced back to you personally:

  • Anonymized abuse reports (reason and timestamp only) — 12 months, then deleted
  • Hashed identifiers (SHA-256 email hash, device ID) — indefinite, to prevent repeat abuse; not reversible to your identity in-app
  • Security logs (e.g., IP on account deletion) — 6 months, then deleted

These retained logs contain no nickname, task text, or chat content tied to your identity.

8. Children

Not for users under 13. Users 13–17 need parental consent.

9. Security

We use industry-standard measures including TLS in transit. No system is 100% secure. We do not claim end-to-end encryption.

10. Changes & Contact

We may update this Policy. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
